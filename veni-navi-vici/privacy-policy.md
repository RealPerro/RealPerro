# Privacy Policy — Veni Navi Vici

**Effective date:** July 10, 2026

Veni Navi Vici is a standalone Apple Watch app for tracking water-sports
sessions (sailing, windsurfing, stand-up paddling, and kayaking).

## The short version

**We collect nothing.** Veni Navi Vici has no servers, no accounts, no
analytics, no ads, and no third-party code. The app contains no networking
code at all — your data physically cannot reach us, and it never leaves
your devices except through Apple services you control.

## Data the app uses on your device

To do its job, the app works with the following data, always with your
permission and only while a workout session is active:

- **Location (GPS).** Used live to show your current speed and heading on
  the watch face, and recorded as a route so your session appears with a
  map in Apple's Fitness app.
- **Health data.** The app records your session as a workout in Apple
  Health, including the GPS route. As part of workout recording, Apple
  Watch measures your heart rate and active energy (calories); these are
  stored by Apple Health, not by us.

## Where your data lives

Everything the app records is stored in **Apple Health on your own
devices**. Apple Health data may sync between your devices via iCloud if
you have enabled that — this is governed entirely by your Apple account
settings and [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/),
not by us. We have no access to it, ever.

## What we never do

- No collection, transmission, sale, or sharing of any personal data
- No analytics or crash reporting
- No advertising or tracking of any kind
- No accounts, sign-ins, or identifiers
- No third-party SDKs

## Your control

- You can revoke Health and Location permissions at any time in the
  Settings and Health apps; the app will simply record less.
- You can view and delete every workout the app has recorded in the
  Apple Health and Fitness apps.
- Deleting the app removes it entirely; workouts already saved to Apple
  Health remain yours to keep or delete there.

## Changes

If a future version of the app ever changes any of the above, this policy
will be updated before that version is released.
